What is Virtual Galapagos?

Virtual Galapagos is an elementary school science software. But it's different from the rest.

Inclusive

Through the inclusion of scientists and ways of teaching from all backgrounds, our #1 desired takeaway is wanting all students to believe that they can do science too!

Immersive

Students are transported into the Galapagos Islands to solve scientific mysteries, traveling through 360 dergee panoramas.

Interactive

Hotspots throughout the panoramas make the tour interactive, allowing students to engage with videos, online activities, and instructions for off-screen activities.


What is a Virtual Tour? Why is it for me?

The immersiveness of our tour, complimented by off-sceen activities, makes VG the perfect tool for science in your classroom.

Education Through Experimentation and Observation

Our tour is broken up into multiple modules, which are each different science units. Each module tracks a storyline, where the user will have to complete activites on and off the screen.

We use a software called 3DVista, which uses Google-street-view-esqe 360 degree panoramas. Students have the opportunity to be surrounded by volcanoes, caves, beaches, and more, allowing for observations of far away places.
