For Students, by Students

Virtual Galapagos is a summer research project at Colgate University, bringing in students to create everything you see.

Who are we?

The Virtual Galapagos team consists of students and teachers from Colgate and Utica Universities in Central New York.

Instructors guide students of all ages in the learning of educational pedagogies, creation of teaching tools, and let students' creativity and skills shine through graphic, web, video, and lesson design.

Our tour inspires students to pursue careers in science, while also training future science teachers and designers!

Check out our Tour!


What is Virtual Galapagos?

Virtual Galapagos is an elementary school science software. But it's different from the rest.

Inclusive

Through the inclusion of scientists and ways of teaching from all backgrounds, our #1 desired takeaway is wanting all students to believe that they can do science too!

Immersive

Students are transported into the Galapagos Islands to solve scientific mysteries, traveling through 360 dergee panoramas.

Interactive

Hotspots throughout the panoramas make the tour interactive, allowing students to engage with videos, online activities, and instructions for off-screen activities.
